About Me

A Dedicated Legal Professional

I'm Pallab Kumar Gorain, a committed and enthusiastic law student currently pursuing my B.A.LL.B. degree from Imamul Hai Khan Law College, Bokaro. Alongside my academic journey, I am gaining real-time legal experience by practising at the Purulia District Court, West Bengal. Driven by passion, purpose, and professionalism, I've committed myself to learning and contributing to India's justice system with integrity, empathy, and deep-rooted constitutional values.
